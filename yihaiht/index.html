Yihai Hengtong International Trade (Beijing) Co., Ltd

Established in 2019, is a full-service supply chain management company specializing in the export of apparel products.

Leveraging China’s comprehensive and extensive procurement and production resources in the textile and apparel sectors, the company provides global brands with targeted research and development (ODM) and original equipment manufacturing (OEM) services for related products.

Abouts Us

Our core products include winter down jackets, ski suits, autumn and winter outdoor mountaineering clothing, spring and autumn cotton jackets, and trench coats.

For five consecutive years, we have successfully provided full supply chain management services to well-known Russian chain brands such as SNOWQUEEN and O’HARA, as well as renowned retail brands MALINARDI and VESVEN, receiving high recognition from our clients.
