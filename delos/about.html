We create unique residential and multifunctional real estate projects. Our philosophy is based on the principles of studying human needs in combination with a deep understanding of modern technologies, which allows us to create projects in which you want to stay.

the art of creating space for life

Developing luxury real estate is a complex, multi-stage process that requires a deep understanding of the market, knowledge of modern technologies and the ability to anticipate the needs of the target audience.

Every stage of this process is important, from choosing a location to implementing the project.

Delos' approach to development is based on the use of innovative technologies, which allows us to create objects that not only fit into the island’s landscape, but also enrich it, offering new spaces for comfortable living.

It’s much more than just constructing buildings — it’s creating new spaces for living and inspiration.
